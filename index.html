FAQ

Questions, answered

What services do you offer?

Window cleaning (interior & exterior), solar panel cleaning, pigeon proofing & removal, pressure washing, trash bin cleaning, hard water stain removal, plus screens, tracks & sills. Need a combo? We'll bundle it into one quote.

Do you offer pigeon proofing or pigeon removal?

Yes. We install humane bird deterrent mesh and spikes on roofs, solar panels, eaves and ledges to keep pigeons out for good, plus dropping cleanup. We serve Hesperia, Victorville, Apple Valley, Adelanto and the rest of the High Desert.

Do you clean solar panels?

Yes! We use a water-fed pole with purified water for a streak-free, scratch-free clean that helps your panels run at peak efficiency.

What areas do you serve?

All of the High Desert and Inland Empire — Victorville, Hesperia, Apple Valley, Rancho Cucamonga, Fontana, Ontario, San Bernardino, Rialto, Redlands, Highland, Upland, Chino Hills, Eastvale, Corona, Yucaipa, San Jacinto, Adelanto, Oak Hills, Barstow, Phelan, and Lucerne Valley. Not sure if you're in range? Just reach out — we'll let you know.

Do you do commercial jobs?

Absolutely. From storefronts to offices and recurring service, contact us for a custom quote tailored to your property.

How do I book?

Use the quote form above, or call/text 714-559-0300. You can also message us on Facebook or Yelp.
