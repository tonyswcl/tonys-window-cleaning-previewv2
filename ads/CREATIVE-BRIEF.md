## Photography Guidelines

### Before/After Windows
1. Take "before" photo from outside, same spot, good light
2. Clean thoroughly
3. Take "after" from exact same angle
4. Crop to show the transformation clearly
5. No filters needed — natural light works best

### Solar Panel Cleaning
1. Show panels visibly dirty (dust/bird droppings) before
2. Show the waterfed pole in action (mid-clean shot)
3. Close-up of clean panel surface after
4. Angle to show reflection clarity if possible

### Video (Reels format — 9:16 vertical)
- 15–30 seconds max
- First 3 seconds must hook: show dirty window → clean result
- No need for voiceover; use text overlays
- Add phone number as text at end: "Call/text 714-559-0300"

## Creative Messaging by Service

### Residential Windows
**Hook options:**
- "Your neighbors just got their windows done. Ready for yours?"
- "Before & after — this is what we do every day"
- "Streak-free windows. One call."

**Body:** Local, trusted, free quote, easy to reach

**CTA:** "Call or text 714-559-0300"

### Solar Panel Cleaning
**Hook options:**
- "Dirty solar panels = lost money every day"
- "When did you last clean your solar panels?"
- "We clean solar panels the right way — purified water, waterfed pole"

**Body:** Efficiency angle (up to 25% loss from dust/debris), specialized method, no chemicals

**CTA:** "Call for a free quote — 714-559-0300"

### Commercial
**Hook options:**
- "First impressions matter. Are your windows making the right one?"
- "We handle commercial properties across the Inland Empire"

**Body:** Reliable, licensed & insured, custom quotes, regular scheduling available

**CTA:** "Contact us for a commercial quote"
