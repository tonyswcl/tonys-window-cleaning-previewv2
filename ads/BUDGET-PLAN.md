## Unit Economics Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| LSA CPL | <$50 (window cleaning benchmark) |
| Meta CPL | <$35 (local awareness / lead form) |
| Booked job rate | 35–40% of leads |
| Average job value | Track (estimate $150–$400 residential, $500+ commercial) |
| Cost per booked job | <$100 |
| Target revenue per $1 spent | 5:1 (i.e., $500 spend → $2,500+ in jobs) |

---

## Scaling Rules

**Scale up (20% budget increase) when:**
- LSA CPL drops below $35 consistently for 2 weeks
- Meta CPL for solar leads below $25 for 2 weeks
- Booking rate exceeds 40%

**Kill / pause when (3x Rule):**
- Any Meta ad set spends 3× target CPL with zero leads → pause immediately
- Any LSA lead category generating <1 lead per $150 spent → dispute leads or adjust categories

**Do NOT scale yet:**
- Google Search PPC — budget too thin; wait until $1,500+/mo
- TikTok — demographics skew young for homeowner services; revisit at $2,000+/mo
- Microsoft/Bing — lower volume but worthwhile at $1,500+/mo (easy Google import)
