# Tony's Window Cleaning — Paid Advertising Strategy

**Business:** Tony's Window Cleaning
**Service Area:** High Desert & Inland Empire, CA (Victorville/Hesperia priority)
**Phone:** 714-559-0300
**Services:** Residential window cleaning · Commercial window cleaning · Solar panel cleaning
**Monthly Budget:** $500–$1,000
**Primary Goal:** Maximize lead volume across all three service types
**Plan Date:** June 2026

---

## Situation Analysis

### Current State
- Active on Facebook (organic + paid)
- Listed on Yelp (review profile present)
- No Google Ads / Google LSA presence
- Website is a simple single-page HTML site — strong for calls, weak for form leads

### Core Opportunity
Tony's has zero paid Google presence. Every competitor bidding on "window cleaning Victorville" or "solar panel cleaning Hesperia" is capturing the highest-intent leads in the market. Google Local Services Ads (LSA) is the single highest-ROI move available at this budget — it carries the **Google Guaranteed** badge, costs $25–$75 per verified lead (vs $90+ for Search PPC), and places above all organic and paid results on mobile.

### Competitive Context
Local window cleaning is low-competition on Google LSA in the High Desert — large national franchises don't dominate this market, making LSA an unusually accessible channel. The Inland Empire is more competitive but still within reach at this budget given solar panel cleaning is a differentiated service.

---

## Strategic Priorities

### Priority 1 — Get on Google LSA Immediately
Google LSA is pay-per-lead, not pay-per-click. Tony only pays when a verified lead calls or messages. At $25–$50/lead for window cleaning, a $400 LSA budget generates 8–16 booked-job opportunities per month. This is the single best dollar in local service advertising.

### Priority 2 — Optimize Existing Facebook Spend
Facebook is already active but likely running without a structured retargeting or offer strategy. Reallocating even $150–$200/mo into a tight radius retargeting + seasonal offer campaign will compound LSA results by re-engaging people who found Tony's through Google but didn't convert immediately.

### Priority 3 — Solar Panel Cleaning as Differentiator
Solar panel cleaning is a high-margin, low-competition keyword category. Window cleaning companies that rank on solar panel terms capture jobs competitors don't even bid on. This service should have its own LSA category and dedicated ad copy.

### Priority 4 — Hold Google Search PPC Until Budget Grows
Google Search PPC (manual keyword bidding) requires $800–$1,000+/mo to generate enough data to optimize effectively. At the current budget, LSA delivers better CPL with less management overhead. Revisit Search PPC at $1,500+/mo.

## Messaging Framework

### Core Value Propositions
1. **Local & trusted** — locally owned, serving High Desert & IE, real reviews on Yelp
2. **Full-service** — residential, commercial, and solar panels (one call covers everything)
3. **Specialized solar cleaning** — waterfed pole + purified water = streak-free, safe for panels
4. **Easy to reach** — call or text, Facebook, Yelp (low friction)

### Audience Segments
| Segment | Platform | Message Angle |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Homeowners (25–65) | Meta + LSA | "Before & After" transformation, streak-free results, free quote |
| Solar panel owners | LSA + Meta | Efficiency loss from dirty panels, specialized cleaning method |
| Property managers / commercial | LSA | Volume pricing, reliable scheduling, licensed & insured |
| Past visitors / engaged FB users | Meta retargeting | Offer-driven: "$20 off first service", seasonal reminder |
