# Implementation Roadmap — Tony's Window Cleaning

**Start date:** ASAP (every week without LSA = leads going to competitors)

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1–2)
*Goal: Get Google infrastructure in place before spending a dollar on ads*

### Week 1 — Google Setup
- [ ] **Claim & verify Google Business Profile** (business.google.com)
  - Add all services, service areas, hours, photos
  - This is required before LSA will approve you
- [ ] **Apply for Google Local Services Ads** (ads.google.com/local-services-ads)
  - Select "Window Cleaning" as business type
  - Upload: business license, proof of insurance, pass background check
  - Typical approval time: 3–7 business days
- [ ] **Gather 5+ Google reviews** — ask your best existing customers to leave a review
  - Reviews directly improve LSA ranking and conversion rate
  - Text customers: "Hey [name], would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps! [link]"

### Week 1 — Meta Cleanup
- [ ] **Audit existing Facebook ads** — pause any that have no clear objective or tracking
- [ ] **Install Meta Pixel** on website (see TRACKING-SETUP.md)
- [ ] **Add click-to-call tracking** on every phone number link

### Week 2 — Creative Production
- [ ] **Shoot 5 before/after photos** (windows — residential jobs)
- [ ] **Shoot 2–3 before/after photos** (solar panels if job available)
- [ ] **Shoot 1 short video** (15–30s) of window cleaning in action
- [ ] **Take team/truck photo** at a job site
- [ ] Set up lead tracking spreadsheet

---

## Phase 2: Launch (Week 3–4)
*Goal: Get first LSA leads flowing; launch structured Meta campaigns*

### Week 3 — Go Live
- [ ] **LSA approved and live** — set max CPL at $60, budget $350/mo
  - Verify Google Guaranteed badge is showing in your listing
  - Set business hours accurately (leads outside hours still charge if you don't respond within 48 hrs)
- [ ] **Launch Meta Campaign 1** — Local Awareness, Residential
  - Ad Set 1A: Victorville/Hesperia 25-mile radius, $5/day
  - Creative: best before/after photo + "Free quote" copy
- [ ] **Launch Meta Campaign 2** — Solar Panel Owners
  - Ad Set 2A: 30-mile radius, solar interest targeting, $3/day

### Week 4 — Monitor & Verify
- [ ] Check LSA dashboard daily — respond to every lead within 1 hour
- [ ] Verify Meta Pixel is firing on phone number clicks (use Facebook Pixel Helper Chrome extension)
- [ ] Check Meta ad delivery — is it spending? Are there any policy flags?
- [ ] Log every inbound lead in spreadsheet with source

---

## Phase 3: Optimize (Weeks 5–8)
*Goal: Lower CPL, identify best-performing creative, build retargeting audience*

### Week 5–6
- [ ] Review LSA leads: dispute any invalid ones (wrong service, outside area, no answer voicemail)
- [ ] Review Meta CTR: pause any ad with CTR below 0.8%; test new creative
- [ ] By now you should have 50+ website visitors — **launch Meta retargeting campaign** ($2/day)
  - Audience: website visitors (30 days) + FB/IG page engagers (60 days)
  - Creative: offer-driven ("$20 off your first clean")
- [ ] Ask 3–5 more customers for Google reviews (reviews compound LSA ranking)

### Week 7–8
- [ ] Increase LSA budget by 20% if CPL < $50 and you're booking jobs profitably
- [ ] Test second Meta creative for residential (video vs photo)
- [ ] Evaluate: is solar panel ad set generating leads? If CPL > $60 for 2 weeks, pause and test new copy
- [ ] Check booking rate: are you converting 35%+ of leads to booked jobs?

---

## Phase 4: Scale (Month 3–6)
*Goal: Grow to $1,000/mo budget, expand service area coverage, add Google Search PPC*

### Month 3
- [ ] Scale LSA to $550/mo (target 14–22 leads/mo)
- [ ] Scale Meta winners per 20% rule
- [ ] Start tracking cost per *booked job* (not just cost per lead)
- [ ] Consider adding dedicated landing page for solar panel cleaning (improves Meta Quality Score)

### Month 4–5
- [ ] If booking 15+ jobs/mo, increase budget to $1,000/mo
- [ ] Add Microsoft/Bing Ads — import Google LSA keywords (3–4 hrs of setup, $100/mo budget)
- [ ] Request commercial quotes page on website to improve commercial lead conversion

### Month 6
- [ ] Evaluate Google Search PPC if budget reaches $1,500+/mo
  - Campaigns: "window cleaning [city]", "solar panel cleaning [city]", brand terms
  - Bid: Maximize Clicks to start, shift to Target CPA at 30+ conversions
- [ ] Quarterly review: compare cost per booked job across all channels; reallocate to winners
